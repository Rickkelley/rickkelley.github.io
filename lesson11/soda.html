Soda Springs Attractions

When visiting Soda Springs, there are two places you must see. The first is the natural soda water spring up at Hooper Springs Park. Come and fill your cup with the naturally carbonated water, or just enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

The other must see, would be the Geyser. Soda Springs is home to the worlds only captive geyser. Similar to Old Faithful, but this one goes off once an hour at the top of every hour. Shooting plumes of water 70 fee in the air, it is worth stopping by. Check out this YouTube video, submitted by a recent tourist.
