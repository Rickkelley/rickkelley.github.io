For the color-scheme of my web page the main body background I kept white. For contrast purposes, and since the purpose of the web page is informational, white to me seemed more professional.

For the header & footer I made the background a golden color, the offical calling number is "#e6e600" then the text within the header and footer, as well as the body is black.

For the menu, or NAV area, of the pages I choose the background color of black. That provides a good contrast against the golden color above and the white below. Since the background is black, to me the obvious choice for font color within the menu was white. However, when you hover the mouse cursor over your menu selection that area background changes to a gray color-scheme and then the font is blue.
