Weather page plan

This site will be www.areaweather.us, which according to "HostGator" at https://www.hostgator.com/domains that domain is available

The logo chosen for our site is the sun, partially obscured by some clouds, and is featured just above this paragrah

Our purpose will be to first provide local weather, which the weather forecast will be updated at a minimum of daily. But then we will also feature a community calendar, a little bit of information about some of the tourist attractions in the area, as well as weather related stories.

For the community calendar we are going to list as welcoming input from those who live in the area as to topics of interest to our neighbors coming up.
