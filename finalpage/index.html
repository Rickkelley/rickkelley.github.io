We run rivers to relax


That doesn't mean we don't like rapids. Opposites in all things after all. We believe that the tenseness that comes when you are looking down the throat of a big string of waves makes you enjoy the calm beautiful water that always follows even more.

We float the river for the beauty

As you are floating your way down the mighty Salmon river, take some time to enjoy all the beauty of it from a different perspective. You will love the spectacular views that can only be seen from the river.

You may also spy many kinds of wildlife along the way like Elk, deer, bears, mountain goats, and of course birds. The Salmon river is home to numerous different types of birds, like the Owl, and the Bald Eagle. It also boasts the densest population of Lewis Woodpecker in the entire state.

Come run with us

We invite you to come and bring the whole family, and enjoy some time with us on a truly amazing river. We have guided tour packages on our rafts for all ages, with trips ranging from a few hours to a few days. If kayaking is more your style then we offer that as well. Since the Salmon river boasts rapids of all classes, you can choose exactly how exciting of an adventure fits you and your family the best.
