Welcome to Salmon River Adventures. Home of what we feel is some of the best rafting in the world. We have guided tour packages on our rafts for all ages. If kayaking is more your style then we offer that as well. The Salmon river boasts rapids of all classes, so you pick how exciting you would like your adventure to be. We have packages ranging from just an afternoon to a full week on the river. For those who prefer to go it alone we offer rentals as well, contact us for rental information. Please check out our tours section, and then pick the option that fits you and your family the best.
